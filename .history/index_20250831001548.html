Our Story

Founded in 2010, Capital City Contractors began as a small family business with a simple mission: to provide exceptional contracting services with integrity, quality, and reliability. What started as a two-person operation has grown into a trusted Ottawa contractor team serving the entire Ottawa metropolitan area with home renovations, kitchen renovations, and basement renovations.

Over the years, we've completed hundreds of projects, from small residential repairs to large commercial renovations. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has earned us a reputation as one of the most trusted contracting companies in the region.
