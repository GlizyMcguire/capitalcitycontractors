How We Approach The Work

Capital City Contractors supports residential and commercial projects across Ottawa with painting, drywall repair, taping, flooring, and renovation work. The focus is straightforward: understand the scope, prepare surfaces properly, protect the space, and leave behind a clean finish.

Some jobs are single-room refreshes. Others are part of a larger renovation. In both cases, the goal stays the same: practical communication, tidy jobsite habits, and workmanship that matches the scope you approved.
