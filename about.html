How We Work

Capital City Contractors supports homeowners, landlords, and commercial clients across Ottawa with painting, drywall repair, taping, flooring, and renovation work. The job starts with understanding the scope clearly so the estimate and the finished work stay aligned.

We focus on the parts that matter on real jobsites: protecting occupied spaces, preparing surfaces properly, sequencing the work carefully, and leaving the site clean when the job is complete.

What We Focus On

These are the jobsite habits we want the website and the work to communicate clearly.

Clear Estimates

The scope, sequence, and expectations should be discussed before the job starts.

Space Protection

Masking, covering, and dust control matter when the property is occupied.

Prep-First Work

Surface repair, sanding, and setup are part of how clean finishes are achieved.

Straight Communication

Questions, changes, and site conditions should be addressed clearly and early.

Clean Finish

Cleanup and closeout matter just as much as the visible final coat or repair.
