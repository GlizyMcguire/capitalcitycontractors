The Challenge: A Kitchen Stuck in Time

Sarah and Mike's kitchen suffered from common issues we see in many Ottawa homes built in the 1990s:

  • Dark, honey-oak cabinets that made the space feel cramped
  • Outdated brass hardware throughout
  • Poor lighting that created shadows and dark corners
  • Worn countertops that showed years of use

Our Solution: Strategic Updates for Maximum Impact

Rather than a complete gut renovation, we focused on high-impact changes that would transform the space while staying within their budget:

Project Details

Cabinet Painting

Transformed dark oak to crisp white

Hardware Update

Modern brushed nickel handles

Lighting Upgrade

Under-cabinet LED lighting

Timeline

Completed in just 2 days
